TikTok Ads Planning

📝 Story

Brand: Fin Your Beauty

Goal: Product Sales

For this campaign, the main focus was to increase product sales for Fin Your Beauty through TikTok Ads. This project is more of an exercise but still driven by a deep understanding of the target audience and effective strategies to capture their attention.


Process:
  • 🔍 Research TikTok Studio Content: Before planning the campaign, I conducted research using TikTok Studio to understand the trends and successful content in the beauty category. By analyzing popular content and its engagement, I identified the approach that would best resonate with the Fin Your Beauty audience.
  • 🏅 Competitor Analysis: I also reviewed how competitors were advertising on TikTok to gain insights into what works and what doesn’t.
🎯 Goals

💰 Product Sales: The primary objective of this campaign is to increase product sales for Fin Your Beauty through engaging TikTok ads. The campaign is designed to encourage the audience to make direct purchases or raise awareness, making them more likely to purchase the product in the long term.

📊 Strategy

💡 Unique Selling Point (USP): I used an approach that highlights the USP of Fin Your Beauty products, focusing on the specific benefits that differentiate the products from competitors.

🎯 Target Audience: Based on the data from TikTok content history, I determined the most relevant audience for this product.

📈 Results

Since this is an exercise, the results presented in the portfolio are the ad structure planning, which includes:

  • 💸 Bidding Setup: I set up a daily budget and used the CPC (Cost per Click) bidding strategy to maximize interaction and conversion.
