📊 Koala Skincare - SEM & GDN Ads Portfolio

📖 Story

Koala Skincare is a skincare brand specializing in products for oily and acne-prone skin. To enhance digital visibility and reach, we executed SEM and GDN campaigns via Google Ads. These campaigns aimed to drive website traffic and strengthen brand awareness among relevant target audiences.



🎯 Goals

  • 📌 SEM (Search Engine Marketing): Increase website traffic through optimized keyword strategies and bidding.
  • 📌 GDN (Google Display Network): Enhance brand awareness through engaging visual ads and effective targeting strategies.

🔍 SEM Strategy & Implementation

💡 Strategy

  • 🔑 Keyword research to identify high-relevance and high-volume keywords.
  • 🚫 Negative keyword optimization to filter irrelevant traffic.
  • 🗂 Campaign structuring based on match types (broad, phrase, exact).
  • 📈 Bid adjustments based on location and device to maximize ad performance.

📊 Results

  • ⚠️ Keyword optimization efforts did not significantly improve metrics after pausing low-performing keywords, highlighting the need for better keyword selection and negative keywords.
  • 📱 Bid adjustments on devices did not yield significant performance improvements.
  • 🌍 In Jakarta, a 5% bid adjustment led to increased traffic, while other cities showed no significant improvement despite higher bid adjustments.

🖼 GDN Strategy & Implementation

💡 Strategy

  • 🌍 Location, device, and time-based targeting for improved campaign effectiveness.
  • 🎥 Using a mix of images and videos to boost engagement.
  • 📈 Bid adjustments to refine ad reach.

📊 Results

  • 📲 5-10% bid adjustment on tablets improved visibility.
  • 📍 5% bid adjustment in selected locations expanded audience reach.
  • 📅 5% bid adjustment on Wednesdays led to better performance.
  • 🔄 Regular creative asset updates (images & videos) prevented audience fatigue.

✅ Key Takeaways

  • 🔍 SEM keyword and negative keyword optimization require continuous evaluation for better traffic results.
  • 📱 Bid adjustments on devices may not always be effective and need further testing.
  • 📍 Location-based bid adjustments were effective in Jakarta but not in other cities, requiring additional optimization strategies.
  • 🔄 Regular creative updates are essential to keep the audience engaged with GDN ads.
